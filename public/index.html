About the Livingston Community Center

The Livingston Community Center offers a space for anyone to come and learn. We offer pre-school services, after-school courses for students grades K-12, and continuing education courses for students of all ages. Visit us at 123 Main St, Livingston, NJ 07039. We're open 8:00 am to 10:00 pm, Monday through Saturday.
